A Day in the Life of a Pikachu

What came first: the chicken or the egg? Actually is was a Pokeball!

Alas! Pikachu was born!

Day by day he gains strength. He is learning how to crawl and strike lightning bolts.

The more he interacts with his fellow pokemon the more friendly and social he gets. What a rambunctious Pikachu!

A growing Pikachu needs to be sure to eat all of his fruits and veggies! An apple a day keeps Dr. Moss away

Regardless of Pikachu's state he always pulls through to be the same happy energetic Pokemon the vey next day.
